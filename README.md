### 📊 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard — Power BI

> A fully interactive Power BI dashboard analysing ecommerce sales data across India. Tracks **₹4,38,547** in total revenue, **5,615** units sold, **₹37,009** profit, and **₹120.9** avg order value — broken down by month, state, category, payment mode, sub-category, and customer.

`Power BI` `DAX` `ETL` `Data Modeling` `Dashboard Design`

![Ecommerce Dashboard](ecommerce_dashboard_preview.png)

**Key Insights surfaced:**
- 📈 Profit peaks in **December** — clear seasonal trend
- 🖨️ **Printers** are the highest-profit sub-category (₹8,921)
- 🗺️ **Madhya Pradesh** leads all states in revenue (₹1,02,804)
- 💳 **COD (44%)** is the dominant payment mode — potential UPI growth opportunity
